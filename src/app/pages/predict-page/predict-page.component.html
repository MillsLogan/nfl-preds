Predictions Help

Layout

Above the schedule will display the current team's name and the record you have predicted for them. The team on the right is the home team and the team on the left is the away team. The team that is bolded is the team's schedule that you are currently looking at.

How to Predict

To make a prediction simply go through the team schedule and click on the name of the team that you think will win the game.

Saving Predictions

After you've gone through each team, and predicted every game, just press the save predictions button and a file will be downloaded. This file will contain all of your predictions for the season, send this file to Logan to be entered into the system.
